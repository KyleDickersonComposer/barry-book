# All the Little Things

### Rough Outline
This chapter will go through some of the common melodic modules that Barry would add to his tunes. We are going with this the term *little things* instead of *melodic module* because we find that term to be tedius and horrible. And because Barry would often say "you got to learn the little things".

We will stick these totally to the beat in order to keep them simple for starters its important to note that this material comes before the stuff where we talk about rhythm in the context of linear improv.

We will talk about how its easier to focus on one thing at a time while learning and trying your best to use the thing in as many ways as possible in the tune.
