# Movements

### Rough Outline
After having introduced all the kinds of chords in the music fundmantals chapter, we need to show common movements and the kinds of chord sequences that show up repeatedly in general.

We are using the term movement instead of chord progression or chord succession because those terms imply that things have to progress. In jazz, we care more about functional harmony and practical movement than about thinking in terms of cadences.
